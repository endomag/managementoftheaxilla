Management of the Axilla

How New Technology is reshaping the 'Gold Standard'

A multi-disciplinary meeting to discuss the latest innovations in breast cancer care

    Thursday 8th June, 18:30 – 21:30   ·   etc.venues Chancery Lane, WC2A 1HL

🗓️  The meeting

Join us for this inaugural national meeting, where we'll be analysing the the role of lymphatic tracers in sentinel lymph node biopsy, and looking to how we can continue to future-proof breast cancer staging.

The meeting will provide a unique opportunity to network with expert local physcians, promote the highest standards of patient care and hear the latest experiences and data guiding the future direction for lymphatic mapping of breast cancer.
